How It Works

Step 1: Take Our Sleep Quiz

The first step is to take our sleep quiz to find out which swaddles might work for your baby based on their size, their preferences and the climate where you live.

Step 2: Enter Your Personal Information

To get your suggested swaddles, you enter your pesonal information to get results.

Step 3: Buy Your Swaddle Online

Swaddleswap will connect you to the best and easiest retailers to buy one of the suggested swaddles.

Step 4: Take the Quiz Again!

As your child grows and their swaddle needs change, you can take the quiz again.
