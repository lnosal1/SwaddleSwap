How It Works

Step 1: Take Our Sleep Quiz

The first step is to take our sleep quiz to find out which swaddles might work for your baby based on their size, their preferences and the climate where you live.

Step 2: Choose Swaddles to Rent

Once you have your suggested swaddles, you can choose 5 swaddles to rent and try out.

Step 3: Try Before You Buy

Try up to 5 swaddles for 14 days, risk free without committing to swaddles that don't work.

Step 4: Keep What You Like, Return What You Don't

Tell us which swaddles you want to keep and those that you want to return. Return in the prepaid postage. Order more of what you liked!
